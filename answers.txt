Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can be broadly applied to the entertainment industry, encompassing areas like film, television, music, and gaming. For instance, streaming platforms like Netflix or Spotify use data science to analyze user behavior and preferences to provide personalized recommendations. By leveraging data on what content users watch, listen to, or engage with, these platforms can suggest movies, shows, or songs tailored to individual tastes, thereby enhancing user satisfaction and retention.
