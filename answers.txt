Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can be broadly applied to the entertainment industry, ins areas such as film, television, music, and gaming.
For instance, streaming platforms like Netflix or Spotify use data science to analyze user behavior and preferences to
provide personalized recommendations.By leveraging data on what content users watch, listen to, or engage with, these platforms can suggest
movies, shows, or songs tailored to individual tastes, enhancing user satisfaction.
